Michael Mediante

Redefining the relationship between architecture and nature. From the sprawling luxury of the Middle East to the coastal serenity of Zamboanga, Michael's journey is one of relentless precision and artistic tranquility.

Architect & Lead Designer

The Journey

The Desert Blueprint

Michael spent over a decade as a lead architect for premier firms in Dubai and Qatar. Immersed in an environment where budget was secondary to ambition, he mastered the art of "technically rigorous" design. He didn't just build pools; he engineered aquatic sanctuaries in some of the world's most unforgiving climates.

The Local Innovation

Returning to Zamboanga, Michael identified a gap between traditional local landscaping and the international standards of luxury resorts. Mediante Landscape & Pool Design Co. was born from the desire to bridge that gap—bringing global architectural discipline to the intimate scale of home and boutique spaces.
