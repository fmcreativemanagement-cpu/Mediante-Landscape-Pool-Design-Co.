## 2. Homepage Layout (Section-by-Section)
1. **Hero:** Cinematic wide shot of a luxury resort pool (UAE reference) + H1: "Resort-Grade Design, Locally Delivered."
2. **The Pedigree Strip:** Clean logos/names of international projects: *LegoLand Hotel Dubai, Royal Atlantis, Jumeirah Beach.*
3. **The Beach Pool Feature:** Introduction to Zamboanga’s first lagoon-style pool specialty.
4. **Service Pillars:** Trio of cards: Design & Engineering, Construction, Maintenance.
5. **The Founder's Note:** Short bio of Michael Mediante with a link to the full UAE portfolio.
6. **Audience Entry Points:** "For Homeowners," "For Developers," "For Resorts."
7. **The Promise (Social Proof Replacement):** "Bringing 18 Years of Five-Star Standards to Zamboanga Peninsula."
8. **Final CTA:** "Book a Site Consultation" (Primary) / "Request a Partnership Brief" (Secondary).

---

## 3. "The Beach Pool" Landing Page Spec
*   **Concept:** Explain the transition from "Blue Box" pools to "Lagoon Living." 
*   **Visuals:** Diagrams showing the sand-finish beach entry and naturalistic rockwork. 
*   **The 4-Stage Package:**
    1. **Design:** Site analysis and high-end 3D visualization.
    2. **Engineering:** UAE-standard structural integrity and filtration.
    3. **Construction:** Single-contract accountability; no sub-contractor friction.
    4. **Maintenance:** Ensuring the "Five-Star Look" persists for years.
*   **CTA:** "Download the Beach Pool Lookbook" (Lead Magnet) / "Schedule a Site Appraisal."

## 4. Visual Design Direction
| Element | Direction | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Color Palette** | Deep Navy, Sand/Beige, Warm Slate, Gold Accents. | Evokes water, luxury construction, and premium materials. |
| **Typography** | Serif Headings (e.g., Playfair Display) + Sans-Serif Body (Inter). | Sophisticated editorial feel balanced with modern technical clarity. |
| **Imagery** | High-contrast, clean architecture shots + macro textures of stone/plants. | Moves away from "lawn care" toward "luxury architecture." |

---

## 5. Phased Launch Plan
*   **Phase 1 (Pre-Launch):** Focus on founder credentials and the "Beach Pool" promise. Use high-quality 3D renders for the local flagship project.
*   **Phase 2 (Post-First Project):** Add drone footage of the first Zamboanga Beach Pool. Include local testimonials.
*   **Phase 3 (Expansion):** Launch the "Resort Maintenance Portal" for commercial clients and a "Design Journal" blog for SEO.

---

## 6. Technical Recommendation
*   **Platform:** Squarespace or Framer. Both offer high-end templates that support the "Design Studio" aesthetic without custom-coding overhead.
*   **Lead Capture:** Typeform integration for the "Site Appraisal" to pre-qualify high-net-worth vs. commercial leads.
*   **Mobile-First:** Ensure "Click-to-WhatsApp" is prominent, as the local market heavily utilizes chat-based conversion.
